The Why

An open laboratory

The labs are a playground for hard ideas made interactive — gravitational lensing you can orbit, exoplanets rendered from real Kepler data, n-dimensional geometry you can rotate by hand.

Every experiment is built in the open and wired to a tiered AI backend: your local model when you have one, a hosted relay when you don't, and a graceful offline mode when there's neither. No keys ever ship to the browser.
